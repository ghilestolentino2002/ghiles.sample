  • DEFINITION OF TERMS

    • Mangoes


    it is typically used as an ingredient to add natural sweetness,

  • Graham Crackers

    • Graham cracker crumbs are made by crushing graham crackers,
    a type of flat and crisp cookie made with
    graham flour and sweetened with honey.


  • Vanilla Extract

    • refers to a flavoring ingredient commonly used in desserts
    and baked goods. Vanilla extract is a liquid that is made by
    macerating and steeping vanilla beans in alcohol, typically
    ethyl alcohol or a combination of alcohol and water.

  • Condensed Milk

    • Sweetened condensed milk has rich sweet flavour and
    is used widely in recipes for deserts and confectionaries.
    It is commonly used as a topping on top of a chocolate.

  • Heavy Cream

    • rich dairy product that contains a high fat content.
    Heavy cream is also known as heavy whipping cream,
    and it is a key ingredient in many dessert recipes

  • Water

    • Water is an essential ingredient in many recipes,
    including the preparation of the graham cracker
    crust or as part of the process when making the
    sweetened cream layer.

  • Nestle Cream

    • Nestlé Cream is often used to create the creamy and
    luscious layer between the graham crackers and mango slices.
    It is typically whipped or combined with other ingredients
    like sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract
    to make a smooth and sweet cream mixture.

  • Frosty Whip Cream Powder

    • a product that can be reconstituted
    with water to create a whipped cream-like
    topping for desserts.
